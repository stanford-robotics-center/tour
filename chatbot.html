The SRC chatbot has access to a knowledge base of academic articles about Stanford robotics research, so you can ask questions like "What is soft robotics?” or “What are toddler bots?”. You can interact with the chatbot either via audio or via text. If you choose to use audio, it's best to use earbuds with with a built-in microphone so that the SRC chatbot hears only you, and is not distracted by other conversation or sounds. Note that the chatbot can understand a wide variety of languages, so you're welcome to ask your questions in the language of your choice. You can also interrupt the chatbot at any time.
