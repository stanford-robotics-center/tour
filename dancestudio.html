The Dance Studio focuses on the study of human motion. The studio has an OptiTrack motion capture system to track and collect data on human movement patterns, fluidity, balance, and coordination. Researchers can use this data to make robots move more like people do.

Also of note, one of our SRC researchers has created a nine-foot tall interactive robotic hand – called Manifold – that has performed with human dancers and gone on display at the Exploratorium in San Francisco.
